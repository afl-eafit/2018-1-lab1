Introduction

"The suffix automaton of a word x is defined as the minimal determinisltic (non necessarily complete) automaton that recognizes the (finite) set of suffixes of x" [Crochemore and Hancart 2013]. With this automaton one can easily answer a wide range of string problems such as locating all ocurrences of any word y in x, how many times such y occurs in x, the longest prefix of y that is a substring of x, the longest substring of x that occurs twice in x and much more [Crochemore and Hancart 2013]. The flexibility on the type of problems that can be approached with a suffix automaton and its low computational complexity make it a usefull tool to know when working with strings in general.
